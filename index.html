About Us

Where English
Comes Alive

Koven Institute of English is dedicated to creating an interactive and friendly environment where students of all ages build genuine confidence in English. Based in Makedonitissa, Nicosia, we follow the Cambridge English curriculum with experienced native-speaking teachers.

🎓

Cambridge Curriculum

Structured progression from Starters through IGCSE and IELTS, following internationally recognised Cambridge standards.

👥

Small Group Sizes

Maximum 10 students per class ensures every student receives individual attention and plenty of speaking practice.

Interactive Learning

Every classroom equipped with projectors and interactive software for engaging, modern English instruction.
